About Us

Greco Tech and Grafix Solutions is a leading provider of innovative technology and creative graphic design services. Based in Buziga Kampala, Uganda, we are passionate about helping businesses and individuals succeed by delivering high-quality, reliable, and affordable solutions.

Our mission is to be your trusted partner for all things tech and creative. We pride ourselves on our expert team, exceptional customer service, and a commitment to quality that shines through in every project we undertake. Whether you need a computer repaired, a new brand identity, or a stunning marketing brochure, we combine technical precision with creative flair to exceed your expectations. We look forward to working with you and turning your ideas into reality.
